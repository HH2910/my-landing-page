Local Market

Property Market in Perungudi

Perungudi sits at the northern end of the Old Mahabalipuram Road (OMR) IT corridor, one of Chennai's most active real estate belts. With Tidel Park and several major IT companies concentrated in and around the area, Perungudi has seen sustained residential demand from the software sector workforce, leading to a dense mix of apartment complexes, independent houses, and commercial properties.

Apartment values in Perungudi typically range between Rs. 7,000 and Rs. 12,000 per sq ft, with premium gated communities and buildings close to the OMR service road at the higher end. The area benefits from proximity to Sholinganallam, Karapakkam, and the extended Pallavaram–Thoraipakkam Radial Road, which connects it efficiently to other parts of the city.

Given Perungudi's IT-professional resident base, there is high demand for valuation certificates for home loans (many residents are in active EMI cycles or seeking top-up loans), visa applications, and capital gains calculation when reselling apartments purchased in the early OMR growth years.

Indicative Property Rates in Perungudi

The rates below are indicative market reference points. Actual certified valuation depends on exact location, age of construction, floor, condition, approvals, and comparable sales analysis at the time of inspection.

🏢

Apartments

Rs. 7,000 – Rs. 12,000 per sq ft (super built-up area). Premium gated communities on the OMR service road and projects with large amenity areas command the upper end. Older buildings in the interior streets are at the lower range.

🏠

Independent Houses & Plots

Land: Rs. 5,000 – Rs. 10,000+ per sq ft depending on road width, street access, and location relative to OMR. Corner plots and those on wider roads carry a premium. Construction value assessed separately per PWD Schedule of Rates.

These figures are for general reference only and change with market conditions. A registered valuation report uses current comparable sales data and a recognised methodology, not portal estimates or guideline value alone.
