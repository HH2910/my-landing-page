IBBI & Income Tax Registered Valuer. Reports accepted by all major banks, the Income Tax Department, and visa consulates. 2–3 working days.

Income Tax / Capital Gains Valuation

Selling a flat, house, or land in Chennai? You need a registered valuer certificate to compute capital gains under the Income Tax Act. Reports accepted by the IT Department on first submission.

  • Section 50C — fair market value for property sale
  • Section 55 — value as on April 1, 2001 for inherited property
  • Income Tax Registered Valuer (1122/Cat-I/2022-23)
  • IBBI Registered (IBBI/RV/04/2022/15081)
  • Report ready in 2–3 working days

Home Loan / Bank Valuation

Applying for a home loan or mortgage in Chennai? Banks require an independent property valuation from an approved IBBI & Income Tax Registered Valuer before sanctioning your loan.

  • Accepted by SBI, Canara Bank, PNB, Punjab & Sind Bank, UCO Bank, Union Bank, Repco Housing Finance, HUDCO, TIIC & J&K Bank
  • Market value, distress sale value (DSV), and forced sale value (FSV)
  • Physical inspection and measurement
  • Report accepted on first submission
  • Report ready in 2–3 working days

Visa Documentation Valuation

Embassies and consulates require certified proof of property assets in India for visa applications. An IBBI & Income Tax Registered Valuer certificate demonstrates your financial ties to India and is accepted internationally.

  • United Kingdom, Australia, United States
  • Schengen countries and UAE

Inheritance & Estate Valuation

When property is inherited or when jointly-owned family property needs to be divided among heirs, a certified fair market value report is required. Court-accepted, signed, and formatted for legal use.

  • Fair market value for estate division among heirs
  • Partition and succession valuations
  • Section 55 computation for inherited property (capital gains)
  • Wealth declaration for legal proceedings
  • Report ready in 2–3 working days
