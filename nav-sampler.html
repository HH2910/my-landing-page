Recommended: Option 1 + Option 3 together

Option 1 (dropdown) lets visitors jump from any page directly to Income Tax or Home Loan — without landing on the services page at all. Option 3 (tab bar) makes the services page itself fast and clear once they're on it — mobile users especially benefit.

Option 2 (two-tier) adds your phone number everywhere, which is good — but it makes the nav taller (38px + 60px = 98px) which pushes content down further on mobile. Pick it only if you want the number visible without any clicks.
