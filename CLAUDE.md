## 12. Document Requirements per Service

The AI assistant should use these lists to answer "what documents do I need?" questions accurately.

### Plant & Machinery Valuation
- Purchase invoices or import bills for machinery
- Asset register (list of all assets with descriptions)
- Installation date and location of assets
- Any previous valuation reports (if available)
- Technical specifications or brochures (if available)
- Purpose of valuation (e.g., bank loan, insurance, M&A, income tax)

### Land & Building Valuation
- Sale deed or title document
- Encumbrance certificate
- Patta / Chitta (for land in Tamil Nadu) or equivalent revenue records
- Approved building plan (for constructed property)
- Property tax receipts
- Survey sketch / FMB sketch
- Purpose of valuation (e.g., bank loan, income tax, M&A, legal dispute)

### Business Valuation
- Audited financial statements for the last 3 years (P&L, Balance Sheet)
- Latest management accounts (if recent year is unaudited)
- Details of business operations — products, revenue segments, customers
- Asset list (fixed assets, inventory, receivables)
- Existing loans and liabilities
- Shareholding structure
- Purpose of valuation (e.g., M&A, income tax, IBC, SEBI compliance)

### PLI Asset Verification
- List of eligible assets (machinery and equipment) with purchase invoices
- GSTIN and company registration details
- Ministry-prescribed format for the relevant PLI scheme (if available)
- Prior year investment figures (for incremental investment calculation)
- Company's CA-certified balance sheet for the relevant year

### EPCG Certification
- EPCG licence copy
- Import bill of entry for the capital goods imported
- Purchase invoice for the imported machinery
- Installation confirmation (site address, date of installation)
- Company registration and IEC (Importer Exporter Code)
- Any prior Chartered Engineer certificates issued for the licence (if renewal/amendment)

### Chartered Engineer Certification (General)
- Description of the asset or goods requiring certification
- Technical specifications or drawings (if available)
- Purpose of certificate (customs, DGFT, pre-shipment, NCLT, bank, etc.)
- Relevant import/export or regulatory reference number (if applicable)

### Pre-Shipment Inspection
- Proforma invoice or purchase order
- Technical specifications of goods to be inspected
- Packing list (if available)
- Location of goods for inspection
- Buyer/seller details and destination country
