Local Market

Property Market in Nungambakkam

Nungambakkam is one of Chennai's most sought-after residential and commercial localities, home to premium apartments, independent houses, and a high concentration of consulates and diplomatic missions. It sits between Anna Nagar and Egmore, with excellent connectivity to Nungambakkam High Road, Kodambakkam High Road, and the MRTS.

Property values in Nungambakkam are among the highest in Chennai. Apartments in established buildings along Nungambakkam High Road and Khader Nawaz Khan Road typically range between Rs. 12,000 and Rs. 22,000 per sq ft, with premium properties commanding higher rates. Independent houses and bungalows in Cenotaph Road and surrounding streets carry significant land premiums.

For property owners in Nungambakkam, accurate certified valuation is particularly important, whether for computing capital gains on sale, securing a home loan from a bank, supporting a visa application, or settling inheritance. The high property values make small differences in the valuation methodology translate into significant tax or loan implications.
