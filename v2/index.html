PLI Verification Services
Investment verification for Production Linked Incentive scheme claims — across Electronics, Pharmaceuticals, Medical Devices, Automotive, Food Processing, and other eligible sectors. Eligible capital investment certified in ministry-prescribed formats, with full understanding of PLI-specific thresholds, asset eligibility, and DPIIT documentation requirements.
PLI Scheme Capital Investment DPIIT Format Ministry Compliance

EPCG Verification
Export Promotion Capital Goods scheme certification — from import duty exemption verification to export obligation closure.
