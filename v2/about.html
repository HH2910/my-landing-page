About Harihar.S

Twenty years of precision.
Built assignment by assignment.

"A valuation is not a document. It is a professional opinion that a bank, court, or regulator will act on. That opinion must be defensible — in methodology, in credentials, and in the depth of the assessment that underlies it."

Harihar.S has been in practice as a Chartered Engineer and Registered Valuer for over two decades, based in Chennai with pan-India assignment delivery. Over the course of 500+ assignments spanning 50+ industries and 100+ corporate clients, the practice has been built on a single principle: the valuation must hold up under the highest level of scrutiny the regulatory context demands.

What distinguishes Harihar.S: engineering-level understanding of industrial assets, IBBI registration for both Plant & Machinery and Land & Building, Income Tax registration in both valuation categories, and Chartered Engineer recognition for government scheme certifications — all delivered by one practice, with Harihar.S personally accountable for every assignment.

Harihar.S works with Chartered Accountants and audit firms who need a reliable expert for their clients, corporates with recurring asset valuation needs, banks requiring empanelled valuers with industrial depth, and companies navigating government schemes like PLI and EPCG where the quality of certification determines the speed and success of every claim.
