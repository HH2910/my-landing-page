Visa Documentation Valuation

Property Valuation for Visa — Canada, UK, Australia & US — Chennai

IBBI & Income Tax Registered Valuer certificate. Accepted by consulates. Digital report delivery — no office visit needed. Ready in 2–3 working days.

Who Needs This

Applying for a Visa and Own Property in Chennai?

Many embassies and visa consulates require applicants to demonstrate financial stability and strong ties to their home country. A certified property valuation report from an IBBI & Income Tax Registered Valuer is one of the most credible asset documents you can submit — it shows the current market value of your Chennai property as assessed by a government-recognised professional.

This report is commonly requested for:

🇨🇦

Canada

Required for Permanent Residence (Express Entry), Study Permit, Visitor Visa, and Spousal Sponsorship applications to demonstrate assets in India.

🇬🇧

United Kingdom

Property valuation is a supporting document for UK Visitor Visa and Skilled Worker Visa applications — particularly when demonstrating financial ties to India.

🇦🇺

Australia

Student Visa (subclass 500), Visitor Visa, and Skilled Migration applications commonly require proof of assets. A registered valuer certificate is the accepted form.

🇺🇸

United States (B1/B2)

A property valuation report from a registered valuer strengthens your US visitor visa application by demonstrating financial stability and intent to return.

🇪🇺

Schengen Countries

European Schengen visa applications for Germany, France, Italy, Netherlands, and others may require proof of immovable assets in India.

🇦🇪

UAE & Others

UAE residence visa applications and applications for other Gulf countries may also require certified property asset documentation from a registered valuer.

Documents You Need to Share

What to Send Us

These are the property documents you need to share. Your personal identification documents (passport, PAN, Aadhaar) are not required from our side — they are submitted directly to the consulate.

📄

Title Documents

  • Sale deed or title document
  • Encumbrance certificate
  • Patta / Chitta (revenue records)
🏠

Property Records

  • Approved building plan (if constructed)
  • Property tax receipts
  • Survey sketch / FMB sketch
📋

Helpful to Include

  • Visa application type (Canada PR, UK visitor, etc.)
  • Consulate or embassy location
  • Whether a hard copy is needed
